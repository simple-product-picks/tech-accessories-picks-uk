About Simple Product Picks

An independent UK tech accessories review site.

What we do

Simple Product Picks is a UK-based review site focused on USB-C charging accessories — chargers, hubs, cables, power banks and adapters. We research and verify the best options available on Amazon UK so you don't have to spend hours comparing specs.

How we choose products

Every product we recommend meets a strict set of criteria:

  • Minimum 4.2 stars on Amazon UK with a meaningful number of reviews
  • Verified in stock and available with standard UK delivery
  • GBP pricing — we only show prices in pounds, not converted from US listings
  • Real specs checked — wattage, port count, charging standards (GaN, PD, PPS) verified against manufacturer data
  • Value at each tier — we pick a best overall, a best budget, and a best premium so there's a recommendation for every budget

Who writes this site

Simple Product Picks is written and maintained by a UK-based tech enthusiast who has spent years buying, testing and returning USB-C accessories. We know how confusing the market is — chargers that claim 100W but only deliver it on one port, hubs that drop 4K to 30Hz, cables that throttle charging speeds. Our goal is to cut through the noise.

How we stay up to date

We review and update our picks regularly. Prices on Amazon UK change frequently — we check our picks monthly and update pricing and availability as needed. All guides show the last verified date.
