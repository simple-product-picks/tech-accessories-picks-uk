## FAQ

**Q: Will these hubs work with all iPad Pro models?**
A: Yes, these hubs are designed to be compatible with all USB-C enabled iPad Pro models, but always check product specifications for confirmation.

**Q: Can I charge my iPad Pro while using a hub?**
A: Most of the hubs listed support Power Delivery (PD), allowing you to charge your iPad Pro simultaneously while connected to peripherals. Check the PD wattage specification to ensure it meets your needs.

**Q: Do I need a Thunderbolt hub, or is USB-C enough?**
A: For basic connectivity, a USB-C hub is sufficient. However, if you require maximum data transfer speeds and support for multiple high-resolution displays, a Thunderbolt 4 dock like the CalDigit TS4 is recommended.
