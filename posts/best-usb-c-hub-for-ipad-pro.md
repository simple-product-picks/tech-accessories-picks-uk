## FAQ

**Q: Can I charge my iPad Pro while using a hub?**
A: Yes, most modern USB-C hubs support Power Delivery (PD), allowing you to charge your iPad Pro simultaneously while connected to peripherals. Ensure the hub supports at least 60W PD for optimal charging speeds.

**Q: Will these hubs work with all generations of iPad Pro?**
A: Generally yes, but it's always best to check compatibility with your specific iPad Pro model before purchasing.  USB-C is a standard now so most will be compatible.

**Q: What’s the difference between USB-C and Thunderbolt 4?**
A: Thunderbolt 4 offers significantly faster data transfer speeds and higher bandwidth compared to standard USB-C, making it ideal for demanding tasks like video editing or connecting multiple high-resolution displays.
