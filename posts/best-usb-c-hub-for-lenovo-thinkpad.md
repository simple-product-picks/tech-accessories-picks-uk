## FAQ

**Q: Will these hubs work with all Lenovo ThinkPad models?**
A: Most modern ThinkPads with a USB-C port will be compatible, but check your specific model’s specifications to confirm Thunderbolt support if you're considering the CalDigit TS4.

**Q: Can I connect multiple monitors to these hubs?**
A: The Anker and CalDigit hubs support multiple displays; however, resolution and refresh rates depend on your ThinkPad's graphics capabilities and the hub’s specifications.

**Q: What is Power Delivery (PD)?**
A: PD allows the hub to charge your ThinkPad through the USB-C connection while simultaneously powering other connected devices. Higher wattage PD means faster charging.
