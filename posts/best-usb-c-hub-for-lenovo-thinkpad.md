## FAQ

**Q: Will these hubs work with all Lenovo ThinkPad models?**
A: Generally yes, but it's always best to check compatibility with your specific model on the manufacturer’s website. Most modern ThinkPads with USB-C ports will be compatible.

**Q: What is Thunderbolt 4 and why is it important?**
A: Thunderbolt 4 offers significantly faster data transfer speeds (up to 40Gbps), supports dual 8K displays, and provides more power delivery compared to older USB standards. It's ideal for demanding tasks like video editing or large file transfers.

**Q: Can I charge my ThinkPad through the hub?**
A: Many hubs offer Power Delivery (PD) which allows you to charge your laptop while connected. Check the specifications of each hub to ensure it supports sufficient wattage for your ThinkPad model.
