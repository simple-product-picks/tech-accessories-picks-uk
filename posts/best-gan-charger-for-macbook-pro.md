## FAQ

**Q: What does GaN technology mean?**
A: GaN (Gallium Nitride) is a semiconductor material that allows chargers to be smaller, more efficient, and run cooler than traditional silicon-based chargers.

**Q: How many watts do I need for my MacBook Pro?**
A:  The wattage required depends on your MacBook Pro model. 13" models typically need 60W or less, while 16" models benefit from 96W or higher – ideally 140W for the newest models.

**Q: Can I use a GaN charger with other devices?**
A: Yes! GaN chargers are compatible with most USB-C powered devices, including iPhones, iPads, and Android phones.
