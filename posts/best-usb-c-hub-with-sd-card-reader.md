## FAQ

**Q: What is Thunderbolt 4, and do I need it?**
A: Thunderbolt 4 offers significantly faster data transfer speeds than USB-C 3.2. You only *need* it if you regularly work with very large files or require the highest possible performance.

**Q: Will these hubs work with my MacBook Pro (M3)?**
A: Yes, all of these hubs are compatible with MacBooks and other devices using USB-C ports. However, Thunderbolt 4 features will only be fully realised when connected to a Thunderbolt 4 enabled device.

**Q: What SD card reader speed should I look for?**
A: Look for hubs that support UHS-II SD cards for the fastest transfer speeds, especially if you're working with high-resolution photos or videos.
