## FAQ

**Q: What does USB4/Thunderbolt 4 mean?**
A: These are the latest connectivity standards offering significantly faster data transfer speeds and increased power delivery compared to older USB versions.

**Q: Is Power Delivery (PD) important in a hub?**
A: Yes, PD allows the hub to charge your laptop while simultaneously providing connectivity, preventing battery drain.

**Q: What SD card reader speed should I look for?**
A: For professional photography and videography, aim for an SD card reader supporting UHS-II speeds for faster transfer times.
