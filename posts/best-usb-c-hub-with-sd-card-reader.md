## FAQ

**Q: What does UHS-II mean for the SD card reader?**
A: UHS-II (Ultra High Speed Class II) is a standard that allows for significantly faster data transfer speeds from your SD card to the hub, compared to older standards like UHS-I.

**Q: Will these hubs work with my MacBook Pro?**
A: Yes, all of these hubs are compatible with MacBooks and other USB-C enabled devices. However, Thunderbolt docks like the CalDigit TS4 will offer optimal performance with newer MacBook Pros that support Thunderbolt 4.

**Q: Can I connect multiple monitors to these hubs?**
A: The number of supported monitors varies depending on the hub. The CalDigit TS4 supports up to two 6K displays, while others typically support one 4K display. Check the product specifications for details.
