**Q: Are USB-C hubs compatible with all laptops sold in the UK?**
A: Most modern UK laptops use USB-C, however check your laptop's specifications for Thunderbolt 3/4 or USB4 compatibility if you need specific features like high-resolution display output – both Anker and UGREEN models are broadly compatible.

**Q: What’s the difference between a 6-in-1 and a 7-in-1 hub, and do I need Ethernet?**
