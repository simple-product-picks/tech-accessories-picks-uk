## FAQ

**Q: What does 'GaN' mean?**
A: GaN stands for Gallium Nitride, a semiconductor material that allows chargers to be smaller, more efficient, and run cooler than traditional silicon-based chargers.

**Q: Is a 100W charger safe for my devices?**
A: Yes, modern devices are designed to regulate the power they receive. A 100W charger will only deliver the amount of power your device requests.

**Q: Can I use these chargers with UK wall sockets?**
A: Absolutely! All chargers listed come with a standard UK three-pin plug or include an adapter for compatibility.
