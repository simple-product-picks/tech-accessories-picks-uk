## FAQ

**Q: What does 'GaN' mean?**
A: GaN stands for Gallium Nitride – a semiconductor material that allows chargers to be smaller, more efficient and run cooler than traditional silicon-based chargers.

**Q: Will these chargers work with my UK devices?**
A: Yes, all the reviewed chargers come with a built-in UK plug or include a suitable adapter for use in the UK.

**Q: Can I charge my laptop with a 100W GaN charger?**
A: Most modern laptops can be charged via USB-C PD at 100W, but check your laptop's power requirements to ensure compatibility.
