**Q: Will a USB-C hub slow down my Macbook Air M1?**
A: Modern hubs like the Anker USB C Hub (7-in-1) are designed to deliver full speed data transfer and power delivery, so shouldn’t noticeably impact performance if used for typical tasks like connecting an external display or storage. **Q: Do I need a hub with Power Delivery (PD)?**
A: Yes, especially with the M1 Macbook Air! PD allows the hub to charge your laptop while also powering connected devices; hubs like the Anker offering 85W PD are ideal for maintaining battery life during demanding use.
