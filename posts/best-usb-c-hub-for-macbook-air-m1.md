## FAQ

**Q: Will a USB-C hub slow down my MacBook Air M1?**
A: A high-quality hub, like those listed above, shouldn't noticeably impact performance. However, cheaper hubs with poor power delivery can sometimes cause issues.

**Q: Can I connect multiple external displays to my MacBook Air M1 using a hub?**
A: The MacBook Air M1 natively supports one external display. Some Thunderbolt docks (like the CalDigit TS4) can enable dual-display support, but this depends on the dock’s capabilities and macOS settings.

**Q: Do I need a Thunderbolt hub or will a USB-C hub work?**
A: A USB-C hub is sufficient for most users. Thunderbolt hubs offer faster data transfer speeds and more power delivery, but come at a higher price point.
