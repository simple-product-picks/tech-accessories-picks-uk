## FAQ

**Q: Will a USB-C hub slow down my MacBook Air M1?**
A: A high-quality Thunderbolt or USB4 hub shouldn’t noticeably impact performance. However, cheaper hubs with lower bandwidth may cause slowdowns when transferring large files.

**Q: Can I connect multiple monitors to my MacBook Air M1 using a hub?**
A: The MacBook Air M1 natively supports one external display. Some higher-end Thunderbolt docks can enable dual monitor support through DisplayPort daisy chaining, but this isn’t guaranteed.

**Q: Do I need a Thunderbolt hub or will a USB-C hub suffice?**
A: For basic connectivity (USB drives, HDMI), a standard USB-C hub is sufficient. If you require faster data transfer speeds, higher power delivery, or support for multiple high-resolution displays, a Thunderbolt hub is recommended.
