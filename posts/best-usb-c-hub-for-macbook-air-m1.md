## FAQ

**Q: Will any USB-C hub work with my MacBook Air M1?**
A: Most will, but Thunderbolt/USB4 docks offer superior performance if you need faster data transfer or multiple high-resolution displays.

**Q: Do I need a hub with Power Delivery (PD)?**
A: Yes, especially if you want to charge your MacBook Air while using the hub; PD ensures sufficient power is delivered.

**Q: What’s the difference between USB 3.0 and USB 3.1?**
A: USB 3.1 offers faster data transfer speeds than USB 3.0 – look for Gen 1 or Gen 2 to identify the standard.
