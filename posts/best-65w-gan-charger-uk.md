## FAQ

**Q: What does 'GaN' mean?**
A: GaN stands for Gallium Nitride, a semiconductor material that allows chargers to be more efficient and smaller than traditional silicon-based chargers.

**Q: Can these chargers charge my laptop?**
A: Yes, all three recommended chargers support USB Power Delivery (USB-PD) which is compatible with most modern laptops requiring up to 65W of power.

**Q: Are these chargers safe to use with my phone overnight?**
A: Absolutely. All the listed chargers include safety features like overcurrent protection and temperature control, making them safe for overnight charging.
