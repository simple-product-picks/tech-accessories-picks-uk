## FAQ

**Q: Will these hubs work with my MacBook Pro (2025)?**
A: Yes, all three hubs are compatible with recent MacBooks, but the CalDigit TS4 will offer the best performance due to its Thunderbolt 4 support.

**Q: Can I connect a monitor via DisplayPort instead of HDMI?**
A: Some hubs include DisplayPort connections; check the product specifications for details. Adapters from USB-C or HDMI to DisplayPort are also readily available.

**Q: What does "Power Delivery" mean?**
A: Power Delivery (PD) allows the hub to charge your laptop while it's connected, eliminating the need for a separate charger. The wattage indicates how much power can be delivered.
